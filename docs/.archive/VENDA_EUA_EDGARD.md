## Strategic Summary

This document captures the Owner strategy for entering the US and international AEC market through technical production rather than render-only positioning. The core direction is to position Apex/Edgard as a BIM Specialist, Revit Modeler, Permit Set Designer, Residential Construction Documentation specialist and offshore BIM/CAD production partner.

Key ideas preserved:

- Do not start by positioning as an architect in the US.
- Start with CAD/Revit/BIM/permit set/documentation services that US firms already outsource.
- Prioritize Permit Sets, Residential Construction Docs, Revit Modeling, BIM Coordination and Estimating.
- Treat render-only, Instagram-only and aesthetics-only positioning as lower priority.
- Use LinkedIn, portfolio, outreach and American-style positioning to enter the market.
- Build an offshore Brazil-based production team.
- Follow Agency -> Platform -> SaaS: sell technical service first, automate internally, then productize into an AI BIM Operations Platform.
- Avoid overengineering the full enterprise platform before validating a focused commercial beachhead.
