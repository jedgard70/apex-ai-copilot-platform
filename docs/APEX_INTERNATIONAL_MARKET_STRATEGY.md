## Core Thesis

The fastest US/EU entry path is not to position the Owner primarily as an architect in the US. The first commercial wedge should be technical production:

- BIM Specialist
- Revit Modeler
- Permit Set Designer
- Residential Construction Documentation Specialist
- Offshore BIM / CAD Production Partner

The real money is in technical documentation, production, BIM, coordination, detailing, permit workflows, construction documentation and AI-powered project delivery.

Render-only, Instagram-only and aesthetics-only positioning are lower priority. They can support sales, but they should not be the primary market-entry identity.

## Business Model

Apex should present itself as an offshore production partner for US and European architecture, engineering, construction and real estate firms.

The long-term direction is an AI BIM Operations Platform / SaaS for architecture, engineering and construction offices, powered by a scalable Brazil-based production team.
