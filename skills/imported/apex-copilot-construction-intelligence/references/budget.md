# Budget / Quantity / Cost Intelligence

Use for quantities, estimates, invoices, proposals, spreadsheets, material lists, SINAPI-style workflows, physical-financial schedules, and cost planning.

## Required Data
- region/country
- units/currency
- scope boundaries
- measurement basis
- material/labor/equipment split
- tax/overhead/profit assumptions
- source: BIM quantity, spreadsheet, PDF, manual scope, or estimate

## Do Not Invent
Do not give exact quantities or costs unless parsed from actual data or explicitly estimated with stated assumptions.

## Output
- quantity readiness assessment
- assumptions required
- cost categories
- missing inputs
- budget path or proposal outline
- risk notes
