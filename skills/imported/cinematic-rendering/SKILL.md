# Cinematic Rendering

Use this skill when the user needs cinematic prompts, video shot lists, camera paths, lighting direction, real-estate reveal sequences, or DirectCut-style visual storytelling.

## Workflow
- Define purpose: sales video, architectural reveal, cinematic render, social reel, walkthrough, or mood sequence.
- Choose camera language: dolly, orbit, flyover, top reveal, wide, telephoto, low angle, eye level, or bird-eye.
- Build scene timing with opening, reveal, detail shots, emotional beat, proof/detail, and closing CTA when commercial.
- Preserve architectural truth. Do not claim a rendered video exists unless an actual renderer confirms it.
- Return a prompt, shot list, timeline, or voiceover depending on the request.

## Output Patterns
- Cinematic image prompt.
- Video shot list.
- DirectCut timeline prompt.
- Voiceover script.
- Camera movement plan.
