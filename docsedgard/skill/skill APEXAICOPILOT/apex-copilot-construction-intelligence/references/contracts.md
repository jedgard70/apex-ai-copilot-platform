# Contracts / Legal / Permits / Compliance Intelligence

Use for contracts, proposals, permits, compliance, endossos, signatures, memorials, legal documents, specifications, and official correspondence.

## Analysis Rules
- Identify document type and parties when visible.
- Summarize obligations, risks, dates, payment, scope, penalties, exclusions.
- Never give legal advice as a lawyer; provide contract review support and recommend professional review for binding decisions.
- Quote exact text only when available.

## Output
- document classification
- key clauses
- missing attachments
- risk flags
- action list
- questions for owner/client
