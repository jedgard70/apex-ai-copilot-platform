PROMPT DE ZOOM IN. 

Create a cinematic transition between two real images: the first image shows the exterior of a modern house, and the second image shows the interior living room of the same house. The camera performs a smooth, continuous zoom-in motion — as if it moves forward through the entrance and into the room. The movement must be perfectly stable, like a gimbal shot, with no shakes or jumps. Keep the lighting consistent between both scenes, maintain realistic motion blur, and ensure the transition feels natural and immersive, as if it’s one single take from outside to inside.

PROMPT CAIXA CAINDO 

The camera is completely static, in a fixed wide-angle position. From above, a sealed cardboard box suddenly drops into the center of the room and lands on the floor with a realistic bounce. Dust slightly lifts from the ground as it hits. The lighting is soft and natural, as if daylight is entering through large windows. The overall atmosphere is calm, photorealistic, and immersive — as if preparing for something magical to happen next

PROMPT CAIXA ABRINDO E DECORANDO 

Create a cinematic transformation between two real images of the same room: the first image shows an empty modern room with white walls and a bright floor, and the second image shows the same room fully decorated. In the animation, a cardboard box sits in the center of the room. The box begins to shake, then bursts open, releasing home decor items such as pillows, vases, paintings, plants, lamps, a rug, and small furniture. These objects smoothly fly out of the box, float in the air, and assemble themselves naturally into their final positions, transforming the empty room into the decorated one. The camera remains completely fixed and stable, with a wide-angle cinematic perspective. Lighting stays consistent and soft, as if natural daylight fills the space. The motion should feel magical, elegant, and realistic — ending with the beautifully arranged decorated room.
